Healthy recipes for breakfast


Banana oatmeal cookies

INGREDIENTS:

2 ripe bananas
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp cinnamon
pinch of salt
1 cup wholegrain oats
1/2 copped walnuts
1/2 cup raisins

  • Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees.
  • Mash bananas, add peanut butter,
    vanilla extract, cinnamon, sea salt.
  • Add oats, walnuts and raisins.
  • Make round cookies, place on the
    baking sheet.
  • Bake for 25 minutes.
  • Leave it to cool down.

Bounty bar

INGREDIENTS:

Coconut ingredients:
2 cups desiccated coconut
1/4 cup coconut oil, melted 1/2 cup
coconut milk
2 Tbsp honey
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp sea salt
Chocolate ingredients:
1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
1/2 cup peanut butter
4 Tbsp cacao powder
1 tsp pure vanilla extract
1/2 tsp sea salt
3 Tbsp honey

  • Mix together the coconut, coconut oil,
    coconut milk, honey, vanilla and salt.
  • Press into a baking paper lined tin.
  • Place the coconut mixture in the freezer
    for 1 hour to set. Then slice into bars.
  • Make the chocolate by mixing together
    all the ingredients.
  • Carefully dip each coconut bar into the
    chocolate and place on a baking
    paper lined board.
  • Place the bars in the freezer to set for
    another 10 minutes before eating.
  • The bounty bars will keep in the fridge
    for up to 5 days.

Brownies

INGREDIENTS:

30gr coconut flakes
90gr protein powder
90gr almond flour
baking powder
4 Tbsp cacao powder
1/2 tsp all spice
2 Tbsp coconut oil
40-60gr honey
3 ripe bananas
100gr walnuts

  • Pre-heat the oven to 200 degrees.
  • Mash bananas.
  • Chop nuts.
  • Mix all dry ingredients.
  • Mix all wet ingredients.
  • Put all together.
  • Add nuts.
  • Place in a baking tin.
  • Bake for 25 minutes.

Carrot maffins

INGREDIENTS:

1 ripe banana
2 Tbsp honey
2 Tbsp coconut oil
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup grated carrot
1 & 3/4 cup ground almonds
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 Tbsp cinnamon
A pinch of sea salt
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup walnuts

  • Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees.
  • In a bowl mash the banana, add the rest
    of the wet ingredients and mix well.
  • Add the grated carrot and the rest
    of the dry.
    ingredients and fold everything together.
  • Pour the muffin mixture into a lined
    muffin tin.
  • Bake for 25 minutes.

Energy balls

INGREDIENTS:

1 cup almonds
1 cup cashews
1/4 tsp sea salt
1 cup dried apricots
1/2 cup dates
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup desiccated coconut

  • Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees.
  • Roast the almonds and cashews
    in the oven > for 10 minutes
    or until golden.
  • Cover the dates and apricots with
    boiling water. Leave to
    soften for 5 minutes then
    drain all the water out.
  • Place cashew and almonds in a food
    processor and blend into fine crumbs.
  • Add the dates, apricots, vanilla, salt
    and combine.

Homemade muesli

INGREDIENTS:

300gr oats
200gr nuts
100gr sunflower seeds
80gr pumpkin seeds
4 Tbsp honey
250gr raisins
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp sea salt

  • Pre-heat the oven to 150 degrees.
  • Cut the nuts. Mix together all the
    ingredients except the raisins.
  • Place on the baking sheet
  • Add honey
  • Bake for 25 minutes or until it gets golden
    mixing up every 10 minutes
  • Turn the oven off and leave
    muesli to cool down.
  • Add raisins

Muesli bar

INGREDIENTS:

2 ripe bananas
1/2 cup peanut butter
3 Tbsp melted coconut oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped cashews
1/2 cup chopped almonds
1 cup oats
1/2 cup desiccated coconut
1/3 cup pumpkin seeds
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 tsp sea salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup raisins

  • Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees
    and line a baking tin with baking paper.
  • Mash the banana, add peanut butter,
    coconut oil and vanilla until smooth.
  • Add the rest of the ingredients
    and mix well.
  • Tip the mixture into tin and press
    firmly. Bake for 30 minutes.
  • Cut it into bars.
  • Place the bars back in the oven for
    a further 10 minutes
  • Turn the oven off and
    leave the bars in there to cool down.

Overnight Oats

INGREDIENTS:

1 scoop oats
1 scoop protein powder
1.5 scoop nut milk
1 scoop Skyr yogurt
1 Tbsp peanut butter

  • Mix all the dry ingredients.
  • Add milk, yogurt and peanut butter.
    Stir well.
  • Put in a fridge for a night.
  • Put in a microwave for 1.5 minutes.
  • Add some berries on the top.

Pancakes

INGREDIENTS:

2 ripe bananas
1 egg
1 cup milk
3 Tbsp honey
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 cup rice flour
1 tsp coconut oil for frying

  • Mash the banana, add the rest
    of the ingredients.
  • Make the frying pan hot.
  • Add a bit of coconut oil.
  • Place with the table spoon the pancake
    paste on the skillet.
  • Fry for 3-4 minutes.
  • Turn and fry for 2-3 minutes
    or until it is golden
  • Leave it to cool down
